Two-Hat Syndrome

Definition A situation where a product manager simultaneous fulfills two different roles or responsibilities in an organization.

Two Roles

  1. Product Planning: Defining the product vision and strategy
  2. Preject Management: Needs expertise in Market trends, technology, or both to deliver the product on time and within budget

How to mitigate Two-Hat Syndrome

  • Enlist a product management specialist
  • Define roles and responsibilities
  • Avoid confusion and conflict
